Welcome to Vat calculator!

Welcome to our site. We have created this site to help people calculate Vat, and help those to fill out the quarterly Vat returns. We could not find a similar site that allowed users to do this, so we saw this as an opportunity to help!

Below you will find an easy to use calculator which will either remove or add the Vat based on what you have selected. This is followed by a quarterly Sales calculator which will break down your quarterly sales numbers to help fill out Vat returns. Please note, vat is calculated at 20% as per the UK rate.
